Mumbai Police Arrest 58-Year-Old Man for Alleged Rape of Step-Granddaughter

Case Details

  • The Mumbai police have arrested a 58-year-old man under the POCSO Act for allegedly raping his 19-year-old
    step-granddaughter over the past 10 years in Maharashtra.
  • The case was registered based on the complaint of the survivor, with charges including:
    • Sections 376 (2)(F)(N), 354, 354A, 323, 504, 506 of the Indian Penal Code (IPC)
    • Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act

Alleged Crimes

The accused allegedly threatened the victim with death if she shared the ordeal with anyone.
The complainant reported that the accused, who lives in a neighboring house, would come to the house when she was alone and rape her.
The alleged sexual harassment began in 2014, and when the victim objected, the accused would beat her and threaten her with death if she told anyone.

Police Action

The Mumbai police have taken action against the accused, and the case is under investigation.
