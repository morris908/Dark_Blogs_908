Tragic Honor Killing in Uttar Pradesh: Brothers Arrested for Sister's Murder

Two brothers in their early 20s have been arrested in Hardoi district, Uttar Pradesh,
for the brutal murder of their 18-year-old sister, Sangeeta Saini.
The tragic incident occurred because the brothers, Durgesh Saini and Ravi Saini,
disapproved of Sangeeta's relationship with a young man from another community.

Part 1: The Discovery

What Happened:

  • The Attack: On May 30, the brothers attacked Sangeeta with a blunt object.
    When she lost consciousness, they set her on fire.
  • Discovery: Her partially burnt body was found on the roadside in the Atrauli area.
  • Investigation: Hardoi Police conducted a thorough two-week investigation.
    They reviewed 250 hours of footage from 125 CCTV cameras and gathered information from
    informants in 129 villages.
  • Arrest: The investigation led to the arrest of Durgesh and Ravi on June 18.
    The police also recovered the car used in the crime.
Hardoi Superintendent of Police (SP), KC Goswami, provided these details and
highlighted the extensive efforts made to solve this case.

Part 2: Police Investigation Reveals Shocking Details

On the evening of May 30, locals discovered Sangeeta's partially burnt body near
Pawayan in the Atrauli police station area. The nearby vegetation was also
scorched by the fire. Upon being alerted, the police arrived and identified the body
as that of 18-year-old Sangeeta.

The investigation revealed that Sangeeta had a close relationship with a young man
from a different faith. She had even gone to Mumbai with him on May 15, a
relationship her brothers and parents vehemently opposed.

Key Details from the Investigation:

  • CCTV Evidence: Police spotted the two brothers in a van on CCTV footage near the
    crime scene on the day of the murder.
  • Confession: During interrogation, both brothers confessed to murdering their sister.
    They admitted to placing her body in a sack, leaving it by the roadside, and
    setting it on fire.

This tragic case underscores the lengths to which family honor can be distorted, leading to devastating
consequences. The meticulous police work, including reviewing hours of CCTV footage and gathering village
informants, was crucial in bringing the perpetrators to justice.
