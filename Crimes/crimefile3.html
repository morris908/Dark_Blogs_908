Tragic Incident in Pune: Brother-in-Law Arrested for Murder

A shocking incident occurred in Khamgaon, Pune, on Saturday evening, resulting in the tragic loss of a young life. Suraj Bhujbal, a 28-year-old garment shop owner, was brutally murdered by his brother-in-law, Amit Bahirat, 27.

Events Unfold

  • The murder took place inside Bhujbal's shop around 4 pm.
  • Bahirat allegedly assaulted Bhujbal with a sharp weapon, then dragged him out and continued the attack.
  • Following the incident, Bahirat surrendered himself at the Yavat police station.

Family Dispute Leads to Tragedy

According to Pune Rural police, a long-standing family dispute was the root cause of the murder. Bahirat had been married to Bhujbal's sister for over a year, but they had been living separately due to differences.

Investigation Ongoing

The police are currently investigating the case, and Bahirat is in custody. The community is left to grapple with the consequences of a senseless act of violence.
